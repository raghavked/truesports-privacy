Privacy Policy

True Sports Cards & Collectibles · Last updated June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how True Sports Cards & Collectibles ("True Sports Cards," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the True Sports Cards mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

Who We Are

True Sports Cards & Collectibles is a retail shop located at 6661 Stanford Ranch Rd, Suite P, Rocklin, CA 95677. The App is the membership, loyalty, and events companion for our store.

Age Requirement

The App is intended for users who are at least 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the App and provide membership benefits:

  • Phone number — used as your primary account identifier and to send a one-time sign-in code.
  • Email address — used for account verification and important account communications.
  • Name — used to personalize your member profile.
  • Purchase and loyalty history — points balances, rewards, and in-store purchase history associated with your membership, retrieved from our point-of-sale provider.
  • Team preferences — the teams you choose to follow, used to tailor relevant deals and content.
  • Device and notification tokens — used to deliver push notifications you opt into.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your membership account.
  • To apply loyalty points, rewards, and member pricing at our store.
  • To show you events, trade nights, and let you reserve a spot.
  • To send push notifications and updates you have opted into.
  • To personalize deals and content based on the teams you follow.
  • To respond to your requests and provide customer support.

We do not use your information for cross-app or cross-site tracking, and we do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

The App relies on trusted service providers to function. These providers process your information only to provide their services to us:

  • Supabase — authentication and secure account data storage.
  • Square — loyalty points, rewards, and in-store purchase history.
  • Shopify — event ticketing and online shop links.
  • OneSignal — delivery of push notifications.
  • Instagram, YouTube, and our podcast host — to display our latest public media content.

How We Share Information

We share your information only with the service providers listed above to operate the App, or when required by law. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide membership services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see "Your Rights" below).

Your Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how we use it.
  • Request access to or deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information — note that we do not sell your personal information.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including encryption in transit, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data using reasonable measures.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
